Type in Cannabis


Magazine (5.25” x 8”, 10 pages), Fall 2024

Type in Cannabis is a mini magazine focused on the typography used in cannabis packaging With the rise of cannabis businesses following legislation to legalize its use, this magazine is a study of cannabis’ businesses design choices. . I designed the magazine to reflect a “stoner”-style of branding, using green colors and a tacky font. All images are my own and edited in Photoshop.
