6 Word Memoir


Card (2.5” x 3.5”), Fall 2024

This is a card designed to capture my personality, using just six words and an emphasis on text. . I am a massive fan of metal music, and since metalheads are known for being explicit, I used explicit language.I incorporated a red and black color scheme, a palate commonly used in metal branding and used a stereotypical heavy metal font.
