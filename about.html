About Me

Bio

My name is Catie Riel and I am a junior at the University of San Francisco studying Design and UX/UI. I plan to graduate in Spring 2027.

Ever since childhood I have always been drawn to art, which inspired me to pursue the creative industry. Being from LA has exposed me to the injustices affecting the lives of Angelenos, and has sparked my strong commitment to social justice. My goal is to fuse my creative side with activism to design creative solutions to issues affecting all communities. With my studies in UX/UI I also bring accessibility and inclusivity to all my projects. In my spare time you will find me at museums, in nature, or in the pit of a concert.
