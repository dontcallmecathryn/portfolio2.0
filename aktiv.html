Type Series: Aktiv Grotesk


Posters (5.5” x 8.5”, 8.5” x 11”, 8.5” x 11”), Fall 2024

This series of posters consist of various texts only using the type family Aktiv Grotesk and a simple color system. The series consists of a school flyer, a recipe, and a bus schedule. The font paired with the text is meant to be the main focus of each poster, drawing from deep research into the typeface's history. I followed a structured design process in which I initially designed each poster in black and white, to foreground composition and contrast.
